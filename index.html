Taj Mahal
A white marble mausoleum in Agra, built in 1630 by Shah Jahan to honor his wife. It's considered a symbol of Indian history and one of the best examples of Mughal architecture.
Khajuraho Temples
A group of Hindu and Jain temples in Madhya Pradesh known for their artwork and sculptures. The temples were built between 885 AD and 1050 AD by the Chandela dynasty.
Konark Sun Temple
A 13th-century Hindu temple in Odisha, dedicated to the sun god Surya. The temple is known for its intricate artwork and the 100-foot chariot with seven stone horses and 24 wheels.
Golden Temple
Also known as the Sri Harmandir Sahib, this temple in Amritsar, Punjab is known for its golden domes and gold-covered top levels.

Hawa Mahal
Located in the Pink City of Jaipur, the palace of winds is one of the most frequented places by tourists.
Victoria Memorial
A remnant of the British Raj in India in Kolkata. This palace in the land of sweets is dedicated to the memory of Queen Victoria.
Chhatrapati Shivaji Terminus
Probably the most buzzing landmark amongst all located in India. This busy railway station in the city of Mumbai is a World Heritage Site.
